SBI PO 

ABOUT THE EXAM:-  

SBI (State bank Of india is a premier nationalised bank working for the country since 1955 although its roots lies more deep 1806 as Bank Of Calcutta)

The Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) is a recruitment body that was started with an aim to encourage the recruitment and placement of young graduates in public sector banks in India, other than the State Bank of India. It also provides standardized systems for assessment and result processing services to organizations.. 

 

The SBI PO exam is conducted by IBPS for recruitment Probationary Officers in SBI’s

 

EXAM PATTERN:- 

PRELIMINARY EXAM :- 

The preliminary examination has three sections namely Quantitative Aptitude, Reasoning Aptitude, English. The exam lasts for 1 hour, The focus should not be on solving question but keeping time in check is important.

TIME LIMIT: -

Earlier there was a concept of sectional cutoff, every section had a particular cutoff set by normalization. From last year, sectional cutoff has been replaced with sectional timing. Now each section is given a particular time. 

A analysis about the exam can change the way one attempts and can make a difference of at least 5-7 marks very easily. Which is more than enough to clear the cutoff.

Mains Exam:- 

Planning of IBPS Mains is somewhat different from Planning the Preliminary Exaination. Mains Exam along with time management requires some level of intensive brain exercise. Reasoning & Computer Aptitude is the biggest section and has biggest duration, but has weightage just equal to Quantative Aptitude. English and General Awarness both have weightage of 40 each.
